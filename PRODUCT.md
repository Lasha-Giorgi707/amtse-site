# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Procurement managers, site engineers, and owners at Georgian construction companies who need to rent a crane (tower, mobile, hydraulic) for a building project. They evaluate suppliers on safety record, fleet capability, operator certification, and mobilization speed. They read professional Georgian; they are not consumers.

## Product Purpose

Marketing + contact site for ამწე ("Amtse"), a Georgian crane-rental company. The site's job: establish technical credibility fast and convert visits into quote requests (contact form / phone). Success = a site engineer trusts the company enough to send a შეთავაზების მოთხოვნა.

## Brand Personality

Structural, precise, heavy-duty. The voice of an engineering firm, not an ad agency: short declarative sentences, load ratings, certifications. Emotional goal: confidence at height — the feeling of standing under machinery that is maintained, certified, and operated by professionals.

## Anti-references

- Consumer-friendly / playful tool-rental sites (bright yellows, cartoon icons, rounded bubbly UI).
- Generic SaaS landing grammar: hero-metric blocks, identical icon-card grids, uppercase eyebrow labels above every section.
- Machine-translated Georgian; English mixed into visible copy.

## Design Principles

1. **The machine is the hero.** Cinematic dark footage of real crane work carries the page; UI recedes.
2. **Spec-sheet honesty.** Communicate like an engineering datasheet — capacities, heights, certifications — even where values are still draft placeholders.
3. **One amber.** A single sodium-lamp amber accent, used sparingly, against monochrome charcoal/steel. No second accent, ever.
4. **Placeholders are loud.** Unconfirmed business data is visibly bracketed and amber-flagged, never plausible-looking fake data.
5. **Weight over decoration.** Hierarchy from type weight (Mtavruli caps, 700–800) and scale, not from ornaments.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA: ≥4.5:1 body contrast on dark surfaces, keyboard-navigable nav + form, Georgian alt text on all imagery, `prefers-reduced-motion` fallbacks for the scroll-scrub and reveal animations, scroll-scrub degrades to a plain looping video on small screens.
